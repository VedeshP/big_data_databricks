### 1. The Core Theory: The Analytics Journey to ROI
Slides 8-11 break down the philosophical journey of why companies invest millions of dollars into data systems. It is a 5-step pipeline:

*   **Issues:** The business has a problem (e.g., Shrinking Market Share, Customer Defection, Fraud).

*   **Analytics (Stage 2):** Applying tools and models (math, regression, predictive algorithms) to the data.

*   **Actions -> Outcomes:** The business changes its pricing model based on the dashboard, resulting in expanded market share (ROI).
